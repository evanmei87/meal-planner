# Meal Plan Requirements

## Caloric Guidelines
- **Daily Hard Cap:** 2250 kcal/day (strict maximum)
- **Pre-Long Run Exception:** 2500-2700 kcal allowed only on the day immediately preceding the "Long Run" (Saturday)
- **TDEE Calculation:** Use Mifflin-St. Jeor formula as baseline
- 120-180 grams of protein per day

## Protein Limits
- Maximum 3 distinct protein types per week. Chicken thighs and chicken breast are distinct
- Examples: Chicken Thighs, Ground Beef, Tofu (Eggs excluded from count)
- Do not exceed protein diversity beyond the limit

## Vegetable Limits
- Maximum 5 distinct vegetable types per week
- Examples: Cabbage, Mushrooms, Spinach, Bell Peppers, Broccoli
- Do not exceed vegetable diversity beyond the limit

## Grain Restrictions
- Strictly limited to: White Rice, Quinoa, Oatmeal
- No other grain types permitted

## Additional Rules
- Leftover usage must be explicitly marked in the plan
- Web searches for missing macro data must use @Web context wrapper
- All dynamic data must be persisted to state.json
